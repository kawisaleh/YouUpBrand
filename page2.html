Our Values
Euphoria

Our aim is to make dating enjoyable and adventurous for individuals from the ages of 40 and up who are open to attempt new things. At YouUp, middle aged singles can discover new relationships and experiences that bring them a sense of joy and adventure while encouraging spontaneity in a place where dating feels authentic.

Respect

At YouUp, we believe that respect forms the foundation of genuine connections and love. We prioritize creating a space where people treat each other with respect. We show that understanding is essential for forming true relationships and meaningful experiences.

Freedom

We believe in the freedom for individuals to express themselves authentically. We have created a safe and inclusive space, where our members get inspired to be their true selves, as well as expressing their individuality without judgement or fear. We embrace uniqueness and provide a platform for open and honest communication by encouraging our members to connect deeper and build genuine connections.

Diversity

Diversity is at the core of our values and having a diverse team ourselves at YouUp. We prioritize inclusivity and diversity, welcoming individuals of all genders, sexual orientations, cultures and backgrounds. By embracing diversity, we provide a platform where you can connect with people who share your values and interests.

Adventure

We strive to provide our members with new and exciting adventures that bring excitement and joy into their lives. Our aim is to bring an adventurous approach to dating by offering the opportunity for new experiences and allowing our members to experience the trills of connection with like-minded individuals.
