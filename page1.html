About Us


YouUp focuses on building a vibrant community whilst leaving screens back in 2023 - we want you to go back in time. With joyous adventures, unforgettable evenings, and endless activities, find the perfect one whilst you endure nostalgic events. Tired of always taking the initiative in love? We will take that stress from you and allow you to take a step into a skyscraper filled with love and memories while you discover your true self. Thirty floors, thirty chances to meet the one - and if you do not, then do not worry. You will meet like-minded individuals who are also looking to embrace one another, with your first drink on the house!

Take that first step out of your comfort zone, whether you are 40 or 50 years old. Embrace uniqueness, and embrace something new.
